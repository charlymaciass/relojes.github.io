-PROCESS-

.01
EXTRACTION
ARGENTINA, PATAGONIA

The beginning of our production cycle starts among the diferent forestry regions of Argentina, mostly located inside of Patagonia, such as the notorious native Lenga forests of Tierra del Fuego.

.02
WOOD BRUSHING
CASEROS, GREATER BUENOS AIRES

Wood is processed into planks of different thicknesses and dimensions due to its diverse uses between the various industries.

.03
CNC MILLING
SAAVEDRA, BUENOS AIRES

Once we have our planks processed, we initiate the CNC milling phase. With the help of precise machinery we forge the main traits of our 3D design into wood.

.04
SANDING
GRAL. PACHECO, GREATER BUENOS AIRES

The CNC milled pieces start to look like our final product. We bring them inhouse to our workshop where fine sanding is carried out. The aim is to achieve a soft uniform surface, sanding down until the wood grain is completely exposed.

.05
WOOD FINISHING
GRAL. PACHECO, GREATER BUENOS AIRES

Now that the wood has all of its grain exposed and its pores open, we proceed with the application of wax and natural oils. It is essential that the previous step was carried out properly, in order to allow a good abosrtion of oil into the timepiece.

.06
ASSEMBLY
GRAL. PACHECO, GREATER BUENOS AIRES

Following completion of the drying period, our timepiece is ready to come to life. The movement is introduced and watch-hands put into position. Mineral glass seals the front and aluminum covers the back. A small "click" fires off upon the pressing of the crown. A new Lenga is born.

-END OF CYCLE-

A Lenga is BorN

Together with "ReforestArg" NGO we are contributing to the restauration of degraded Patagonian native forests.
With the sale of each product, a new Lenga tree is born.
¿Are you interested in further information about us, and our project?

After an entire year of drafts, prototypes and product reinventions we managed to materialize our vision. It was a 12 month period in which the utmost detail was put to discussion. A period of disconformity that seemed to never end. All with the aim of consolidating our product by design, without discarding the details.

That's how Lengas was forged. With trial, error, and uncountable bus trips among all of Greater Buenos Aires in search of suppliers. Providing today, a beautiful handcrafted product that seeks to represent the most valuable thing in life, time. A watch that integrates the dial, bezel and case in one pure and unique wooden piece. Accompanied by a system that allows us to give back a tree to our native forests, for every watch sold.

We have reached the type of audience we always dreamed of. One that really cares about our mission and the values behind our brand.

We are in love with each part of the process. From the selection of wood in the sawmill to the manufacture, packaging and marketing of the product. We are active participants of each and every step of the cycle. This has allowed us to learn and identify the talent needed to carry out the process, and little by little, start to grow our team.

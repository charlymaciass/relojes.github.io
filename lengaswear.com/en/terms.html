Terms and conditions / FAQ

How do you receive your order?

Just fill out your receiver address in the checkout form. We will dispatch through private courier, it will arrive at your door.

How long does the shipping take? How much does it cost?

From the moment the order is dispatched, it may take approximately 20 days to reach destination. The wait will be worth it. Good things take time. Shipping is free so you dont have to worry about unexpected charges. You will be provided with a tracking code upon the dispatch of your order.

What if there's no one at home to receive the product?

An attempted delivery notice is left. Your order will remain in the nearest branch office. Show up with your tracking number in order to release your order.

Why are there different prices among such similar timepieces?

Each watch is treated with its own different woods and leathers. The difference in wood density will determine the level of work needed to forge the piece. More expensive models will usually count with a more exclusive and harder wood.

Warranty



This 1 year warranty, runs from the day the order is delivered, and covers imperfections that may come from the manufacturing process. It does not cover additional shipping costs involved.

The natural wear of the piece will not be covered by the warranty. Purchase certificate is required to make use of the warranty.

Lengas will not accept warranty claims if the watch has clear evidence of improper use. In case of any doubts, reach out to our support team at hello@lengaswear.com detailing your name and e-mail.

In case the product is delivered with any flaw or defect, reach out to hello@lengaswear.com. We will take responsibility for the reposition of a new unit and all the shipping costs involved.

Buyer warranty will not be available for purchases outside our website.

This warranty will not cover:

-Damage cause by improper use/manipulation of the piece. Accidents, thefts or losses.
-Shipping costs for purchases carried out outside our website.
-Damage caused by water.

This warranty covers:

-Flaws in the placement of watch-hands (movement).
-Flaws in the watch itself (case/bezel/dial).
-Flaws in the assembly of the crystal.
-Logistics (If the watch was manipulated poorly, resulting in a damaged product).

Returns

If you are not at peace with the product you bought, you will be allowed a free return within the first 7 days upon receiving your order. Shipping costs will not be covered by Lengas.

Refund

Once the operation detailed above has taken place, and the package arrives at our destination, the total amount of the order will be returned to the buyer's account.
